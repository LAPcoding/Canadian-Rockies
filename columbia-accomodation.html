Hotels

Glacier View Lodge

Nestled in a high alpine zone dominated by the iconic Columbia Icefield, the Glacier View Lodge is an experience like no other. From the moment you check in, you’re treated to a premium hospitality experience loaded with exclusive activities, unique dining experiences and entertainment. You’ll be inspired. And it’s all just one hour from Jasper.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$399 CAD

Sunwapta Falls Rocky Mountain Lodge

Sunwapta is located 53km south of downtown Jasper on HWY 93, the Icefields Parkway. That puts our Jasper Hotel literally in the heart of Jasper National Park! We’re ideal for people looking to unplug from the regular hectic pace of life, and have more space to focus on loved ones and nature. No cell phone reception, no phones in the rooms, and slow internet (it’s the best we can get).

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$199 CAD

The Crossing Resort

Your true Canadian Rockies experience awaits you here. Come and discover The Crossing Resort on the Icefields Parkway in Banff National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site! Nestled at the base of towering Mt. Wilson, and surrounded by stunning mountain vistas and glaciers, The Crossing Resort boasts a spectacular alpine location unmatched anywhere in North America, within 30 minutes from most major attractions. The Crossing Resort is located 85 kms (51 mi) north of Lake Louise, at the junction of Highway # 93 (the Icefields Parkway), and Highway # 11, the David Thompson Highway.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$159 CAD

Hostels

HI Beauty Creek Wilderness Hostel

Fire up the panoramic setting on your camera. HI Beauty Creek is on the edge of the Sunwapta River, a wide open tract of land and water perfect for spotting caribou, wolves and other wildlife or just for stargazing at night. There's no electricity here, but who needs it.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$38 CAD

HI Rampart Creek Wilderness Hostel

About half way between Banff and Jasper, HI Rampart Creek is a hiker's and climber's paradise with trails just out the door and bolted sport-climbing routes a five-minute walk away. After all that exertion, sit back by the campfire or take a brisk dip in the nearby glacier-fed creek.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$37 CAD

Campgrounds

Icefields (tents only)

Down the road from the world-renowned Columbia Icefield, this campground puts you on the doorstep of some of the most spectacular scenery on the planet. Glacier views are had from most campsites and from the windows of the cozy log cookhouses.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$26 CAD

Icefields Center RV

This is a large asphalt parking lot located on the west end of the Icefields Visitors Centre. There are 100 sites lined out on the asphalt. Unfortunately the entire parking lot is not level and gently slants towards the highway. It’s not intended for long use but does make for a nice rest stop or an overnight stay. If your RV is over 10ms (30ft) this site is the only option if you plan on exploring this area.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$17 CAD

Wilcox Creek Campground

Sitting at the base of one of Jasper's premiere day hikes, with which it shares its name, Wilcox Campground is also down the road from the Columbia Icefield, offering access to a variety of family friendly amenities and day trips. Best suited for motorhomes and trailers under 27 feet

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$26 CAD

Jonas Creek Campground

Jasper's Jonas Creek Campground facilities are basic, and include outhouses, picnic tables and metal fire rings. Less-busy than the campgrounds closer to town, Jonas is also a nice overnight stop for RV’rs or cyclists who prefer a nice quiet place to spend the night.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$26 CAD

Honeymoon Lake Campground

Being able to replace ringing phones, noisy traffic and city smog with quiet lakeside strolls and the smell of bacon cooking over a campfire gives you a sense of how Honeymoon Campground got its name. Best suited for tenting, and motorhomes and trailers under 27 feet

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$26 CAD

Rampart Creek Campground

Rampart Creek Campground offers a secluded and rustic camping experience along the beautiful Icefields Parkway, 40 km from the Columbia Icefield. The campground backs onto the banks of the North Saskatchewan River with impressive views of the river valley and surrounding mountains.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$28 CAD

Backcountry Camping & Cabins

Glacier Lake Campground

A backpacking trip to Glacier Lake is a great early-season objective as well as being popular with novice and intermediate backpackers. Visit one of Banff’s largest lakes on this pretty backpacking route, which can also be squeezed into a long day hike. Located on the Icefields Parkway near Saskatchewan River Crossing.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$10 CAD

Norman Lake Campground

A fairly open campground, good for groups, in mixed forest. Adjacent willow flats mean good views from many parts of the campground. Norman Lake is less than half a kilometer away. Day trips from the campground include Sunset Pass and Sunset Lookout.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$21 CAD

Castleguard

Located near treeline among small fir and spruce, this remote camping area gives you access to the wide open Castleguard Meadows. Because this area is zoned as a special preservation are, camping is limited to an area within 100 meters of the campground sign. Note there is no food storage device at this site.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$21 CAD

Sydney Vallance (Fryatt) Hut

A relatively little-known and little-visited valley, this area is one of the true gems of the Rockies! The Fryatt Hut offers a superb base for exploration both summer and winter with excellent alpine climbing and ski-touring all within fairly easy reach of the cabin.

AVG RATE/NIGHT
$50 CAD
